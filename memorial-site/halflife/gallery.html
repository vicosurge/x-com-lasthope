About These Screenshots

These screenshots were recovered from ModDB and the Internet Archive. They showcase the incredible work done by the Last Hope team in bringing the X-COM universe to Half-Life's first-person perspective.

The mod featured custom aliens, UFOs, weapons, and maps - all designed to recreate the tactical combat experience of the original X-COM games.
