About These Screenshots

These screenshots were recovered from the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine captures of lasthopemod.com from 2002-2003. The original website sections included:

  • Models: Character and object models
  • Weapons: Custom X-COM styled weapons
  • Items: Equipment and pickup items
  • In-Game: Screenshots of maps and gameplay

Unfortunately, many of the original images could not be recovered as the image hosting links are no longer accessible. The descriptions and references preserved here help document what content existed during the mod's development.
