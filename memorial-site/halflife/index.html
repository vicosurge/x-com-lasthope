Half-Life Era

2002 - 2006 | The Original X-COM Inspired Modification

About the Half-Life Mod

Last Hope began as an ambitious modification for Valve's Half-Life, drawing deep inspiration from the beloved X-COM series. The project aimed to bring the tactical alien defense theme of X-COM into the immersive first-person shooter experience of Half-Life.

The mod featured custom weapons modeled after X-COM's iconic arsenal, unique player and enemy models, detailed maps inspired by X-COM mission environments, and items that captured the essence of the strategic gameplay that made X-COM a legend.

Development was centered around the phpBB community forums at lasthopemod.com, where modders from around the world collaborated on mapping, texturing, modelling, and skinning. The forums became a hub for discussion, feedback, and showcasing work-in-progress content.

Planned Features

X-COM Weapons

Custom weapon models based on X-COM's arsenal, including the iconic X-COM Pistol created by community member ApOcaLyPSe_1985.

Custom Maps

Original maps inspired by X-COM mission environments, including farm maps and tactical combat scenarios designed by mappers like XPlicit.

Player & Enemy Models

Custom character models for X-COM soldiers and alien enemies, bringing the tactical combat of X-COM into Half-Life's 3D world.

Items & Equipment

X-COM themed equipment and items to enhance the tactical gameplay experience, from medkits to specialized alien artifacts.
