Team Members

The people who made Last Hope possible

Last Hope was built by a dedicated community of volunteer modders who contributed their time and talent to the project. Below are some of the known contributors whose work was recovered from the archives.

Core Team

The founding members who launched the project on June 14, 2002.

🇺🇸

Salvi

Project Lead / Moderator

Founding Member - Jun 14, 2002

Florida, US. Led the Last Hope project and moderated the Off-topic forum. Built the website backend and coordinated the development team.

🇳🇱

ApOcaLyPSe_1985

Lead Modeller / Weapon Artist

Founding Member - 1,494+ posts

The Netherlands. ICT student. Top contributor and Colonel rank. Created the iconic X-COM weapon models. Interests: X-COM, Science, Computer Hardware, 3D Modelling.

🇨🇦

Psyguy

Forum Moderator

Founding Member - 427+ posts

Montreal, Canada. Moderated General Discussion, Mapping & Texturing, and Modelling & Skinning forums.

To All Contributors

The archived records only preserve a small portion of the community that made Last Hope possible. Many contributors' names have been lost to time, but their work lives on in the screenshots and memories preserved here.

If you were part of the Last Hope community and would like to be recognized, or if you have additional information about the project and its contributors, we would love to hear from you.
