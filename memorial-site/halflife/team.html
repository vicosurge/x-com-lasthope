Team Members

The people who made Last Hope possible

Last Hope was built by a dedicated community of volunteer modders who contributed their time and talent to the project. Below are some of the known contributors whose work was recovered from the archives.

Known Contributors

ApOcaLyPSe_1985

Modeller / Weapon Artist

Created the iconic X-COM Pistol model, establishing the visual style for the mod's weapon arsenal.

XPlicit

Level Designer / Mapper

Created the Farm map and other environments, bringing X-COM mission scenarios to life in Half-Life.

Psyguy

Forum Moderator

Moderated multiple forum sections including General Discussion, Mapping & Texturing, and Modelling & Skinning.

Cyalume

Community Contributor

Active community member who contributed to mapping and modelling discussions.

POCTheSainT

Community Contributor

Active participant in general discussions and community building.

To All Contributors

The archived records only preserve a small portion of the community that made Last Hope possible. Many contributors' names have been lost to time, but their work lives on in the screenshots and memories preserved here.

If you were part of the Last Hope community and would like to be recognized, or if you have additional information about the project and its contributors, we would love to hear from you.
