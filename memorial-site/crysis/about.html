Story & Lore

The World of Last Hope

Introduction

This mod is a total conversion of Crysis. It will feature new enemies, weapons, possibly vehicles, maps, all focusing on fun, class-based objective multiplayer.

This mod is about an alien race called the Slaver Horde, taking over Earth, and its main defense the Earth Scope (a planet-sized shield defence grid). This mod will feature indoor, but mainly outdoor gameplay, and span across from Antarctica, to Africa, to Los Angeles.

Our squad, Omega Squad, of the United Earth Coalition (or UEC), has been tasked with taking over the Earth Scope stations located all around the globe.

Prologue

The year is 2174. With the recent construction of the Earth Scope - a large, planet-wide, orbital shield - humanity has entered a golden age. Our countries have forged new bonds of peace, and brotherhood. War is now in our past, and drugs, violence, prostitution, everything that plagued the 21st century, is now behind us. We all thought this age would last forever... we were wrong.

I guess war is a must in human existence. In the year 2176, an alien race - we've grown to call them, the Slaver Horde - took interest in our planet. At first, they just sat, and watched our planet grow as a community. I don't know what made them want to, but in a matter of months, they declared open war. It was a nightmare. The Horde moved in, they took control of the Scope in only 2 months, and conquered all of our greatest nations.

Now, the year is 2214. Our once great planet Earth is now all but an apocalyptic wasteland. The Horde has taken control of our planet, and we are almost helpless to stop them. Formed from the ashes of many great countries of old, the U.E.C., or United Earth Coalition, has risen up in desperate hope to try and cast down our oppressors.

We have vowed as soldiers of the U.E.C. to reclaim the once great Earth Scope. Only then, can we stop the swarm of the Horde, and give hope to our once great planet.

We are the U.E.C. We are our Last Hope.

Key Factions & Elements

The Earth Scope

A large, planet-wide, orbital shield defense grid constructed by humanity. Its creation ushered in a golden age of peace - until it was captured by the Slaver Horde and turned against its creators.

The Slaver Horde

A mysterious alien race that observed humanity before launching a devastating invasion in 2176. They captured the Earth Scope in just two months and have occupied Earth for nearly 40 years.

United Earth Coalition (UEC)

Formed from the ashes of Earth's fallen nations, the UEC represents humanity's last organized resistance against the Horde occupation. Their mission: reclaim the Earth Scope stations.

Omega Squad

The elite UEC unit at the center of the story. Tasked with the critical mission of capturing Earth Scope stations around the globe, they are humanity's best hope for liberation.

Timeline

  • Story accompanying every unique map

  • Multiple global locations: Antarctica, Africa, Los Angeles
