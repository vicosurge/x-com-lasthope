About This Gallery

These images were recovered from ModDB and archived sources. They represent the ambitious visual design of The Last Hope Crysis mod, including custom vehicles, weapons, and environments built for CryEngine.

The mod aimed to create a complete sci-fi universe with original faction designs, military hardware, and post-apocalyptic environments spanning multiple continents.
