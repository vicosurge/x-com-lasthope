Team Composition

Leadership

George "Blackhawk" Sawday led the project as Project Leader while also serving as Lead Level Designer, guiding the mod's creative direction.

Level Design

A strong team of level designers including Matt "Espilon" Brown, Caspar "Sisp" Wain, and Tony "Gunz" Clarke worked on bringing the post-apocalyptic Earth to life.

Art & Assets

Piotr "Toasteding" Dacewicz handled 3D art while Micha Zimmermann provided concept art. Matt Brown also contributed graphics work alongside his other roles.

Story & Web

Alex "Demise" Jennings crafted the compelling Earth Scope storyline, while Matt Brown developed and maintained the project's website.

An International Effort

The Last Hope Crysis team represented the global nature of modding communities. With team members from multiple countries working together:

  • United Kingdom: George Sawday, Caspar Wain, Tony Clarke, Piotr Dacewicz
  • United States: Matt Brown
  • Germany: Micha Zimmermann
  • Canada: Alex Jennings

This international collaboration was made possible by the internet and shared passion for game modding - a testament to how creative communities transcend borders.

Jobs & Recruitment

The archived website included a jobs/recruitment page, indicating the team was actively seeking additional contributors to help realize their ambitious vision for the mod.

The positions they were looking to fill and the specific skills they sought are a reminder of how much work goes into a total conversion mod - and how community members with various talents were always welcome to join the effort.
